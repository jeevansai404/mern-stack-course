🏋️ Gym Run – Workout Plan

Day 1 – Chest + Triceps (Push Day)

  • Warm-up: 5–10 min light cardio
  • Bench Press – 4 sets x 8–12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3x10
  • Chest Fly (machine/dumbbells) – 3x12
  • Tricep Dips – 3x12
  • Tricep Pushdowns – 3x12
  • Plank – 3 sets (hold 30–60 sec)

Day 2 – Back + Biceps (Pull Day)

  • Deadlift – 4x8
  • Lat Pulldown / Pull-ups – 3x10
  • Barbell Row – 3x10
  • Dumbbell Curl – 3x12
  • Hammer Curl – 3x12
  • Face Pulls (rear delts) – 3x12

Day 3 – Legs + Core

  • Squats – 4x8–10
  • Leg Press – 3x12
  • Lunges – 3x12 (each leg)
  • Leg Curls (hamstrings) – 3x12
  • Calf Raises – 3x15
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3x12

Day 4 – Shoulders + Abs

  • Overhead Press – 4x8–10
  • Lateral Raises – 3x12
  • Front Raises – 3x12
  • Shrugs – 3x15
  • Arnold Press – 3x10
  • Russian Twists – 3x20

Day 5 – HIIT + Functional Training

  • 20–30 min HIIT (treadmill sprints, cycling, rowing, battle ropes)
  • Kettlebell Swings – 3x15
  • Box Jumps – 3x12
  • Burpees – 3x15
  • Farmer’s Carry – 3 rounds (30–40 sec each)
  • Core Circuit (plank, side plank, mountain climbers) – 3 rounds

Day 6 – Full Body Strength (Compound Focus)

  • Deadlift – 4x6–8
  • Bench Press – 4x8
  • Pull-ups – 4x8–10
  • Squats – 4x8
  • Military Press – 3x10
  • Core Finisher: Ab wheel rollouts – 3x10

Day 7 – Rest & Recovery

  • Light walking, stretching, or yoga
  • Focus on hydration, sleep, and recovery

⚡ Tips for Members:

  • Always warm up & cool down (5–10 min each).
  • Adjust weights as per fitness level.
  • Track progress weekly.
  • Pair with the diet plan we made for best results.
